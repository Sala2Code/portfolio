Lambda Coalescent
2025 Nov.

Random simulation of Λ-coalescents, a retrospective generative process for genealogies allowing simultaneous mergers beyond Kingman's binary case. We consider Λ as a probability measure on [0,1], which specifies merger rates of ancestral lineages. Implements and compares Kingman and Beta coalescents, analyzes the TMRCA (Time to Most Recent Common Ancestor), and applies the models to a practical case to assess sweepstakes reproduction and which regime better fits observed trees.
